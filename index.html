FESTRONIX 2K25

National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, K. Ramakrishnan College of Technology, Samayapuram

Register Now View Events

Events

📄

Paper Presentation

Technical
Venue: Circuit Block Second Floor Conference Hall
Coordinator: Mrs. A. Thenmozhi & Mrs. S. Gayathri
  • Maximum 2 members per team.
  • Topics must be related to Computer Science, IT, or Emerging Technologies (AI, IoT, AR/VR, Cybersecurity, etc.).
  • Papers should be prepared in IEEE format.
  • PowerPoint presentation (PPT) is mandatory.
  • Original work earns bonus points.
  • Plagiarism or copied content leads to disqualification.
💡

Ideathon

Technical
Venue: Circuit Block Third Floor LHC 302
Coordinator: Ms. M. Suriya
  • Maximum 3 members per team.
  • Problem statements can be chosen from real-world or tech-based challenges.
  • Teams must explain the problem, proposed solution, and impact.
  • Prototype/demo is optional but adds bonus points.
  • Plagiarism or copied ideas lead to disqualification.
🎬

Video Editing

Technical
Venue: Circuit Block Third Floor Lab-3
Coordinator: Ms. V. Sowmiya
  • Maximum 2 members per team.
  • Any offline editing tool can be used.
  • Final output duration: 1–2 minutes.
  • Offensive or copyrighted content will lead to disqualification.
🔥

Free Fire

Non-Technical
Venue: Circuit Block Third Floor Lab-4 & 5
Coordinator: Mrs. V. Kalpana
  • Registration: ₹250 per head.
  • 2 members per team.
  • Stable internet connection required.
  • No hacks, mods, or unfair means allowed.
🧩

ConnectionX

Non-Technical
Venue: Circuit Block Third Floor THC 301
Coordinator: Mrs. A. Dhivya Bharathi
  • Teams will be shown a series of images or hints related to a word or phrase.
  • Faster answers score higher.
  • No mobile phones or external help allowed.
  • Top teams from prelims qualify for finals.
🎵

TuneSeek

Non-Technical
Venue: Circuit Block Third Floor Lab-1
Coordinator: Mrs. K. Vallipriyadharshini
  • Maximum 2 members per team.
  • Identify songs or background scores played for short durations.
  • Rounds may include humming, reversed audio, or instrumental clues.
  • Operates on a buzzer system.
  • No phones allowed. Time limits strictly followed.

Technical Events — Round 1 (Session 1)

11:00 AM – 11:15 AM

Refreshment Break ☕

11:15 AM – 01:00 PM

Technical Events — Round 1 (Session 2)

Continuation of Paper Presentation, Ideathon, and Video Editing events.

Event Conclusion

FESTRONIX 2K25 concludes with Vote of Thanks.
